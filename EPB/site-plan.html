Overview

Purpose

Need an excuse to shoot your siblings, friends, or parents? Extreme Paintball can help you out! Extreme Paintball is a locally owned paintball arena, that offers inexpensive deals with safe equipment and protective gear for both kids and young aduts. We are all about having fun here at Extreme Paintball and we like to have fun the safe way. We love to provide a safe and enjoyable atmosphere for friends and families on the battlefield.

Audience

Our audience is mostly made up of families and young adults. Young adults in the 21st century, love playing shooter video games such as Call of Duty and Halo. We want to give them a more interactive and physical experience that will give them a chance to enjoy the time out with their friends and to create new memories We always encourage families to come out to the course for a day out on the fields, to enjoy the time spent as a family.

We have two battelfields to play on. we have speedball which is our fast pace no surrender policy course, and the we have Verdun which is a more rural layout. Verdun is a more family friendly layout that is gives you the real battlefield experience. On the battelefield of Verdun, you will encounter a thick and woodsy enviroment. Bring some shoes and clothes that you don't mind getting dirty because there will be a little bit of mud along with a creek that runs through the center of the battlefield. There are two different games to play on both of these maps. You can either play classic team death match or capture the flag. In TDM the rules are simple: once you are hit you are out of the match getting shot in the gun does not mean you are out If you are out of ammo you are out, unless you can be resupplied via teammates In Capture the flag the rules are a little different: There are respawns, which means if you are hit, you have to go to your teams respawn zone to respawn If you are hit while holding the flag of the other team, you must drop it immediatley The flag can't be thrown your team can return your flag if it is stolen and dropped

Verdun map layout and enviroment: Woodsy creek runs through middle of battlefield more family friendly More for casual play may encounter some mud lots of natural cover (trees, bushes, logs) Barricades, barrells, and two two-story forts (one for each team) great for capture the flag Speedball map layout and enviroment: open field with blow up barricades fast pace more for competition and tournaments

Rentals: Paintball gun, mask, and 500 rounds - $40 (Standard rental) Paintball gun, mask, 500 rounds and body armor - $50 (for those who don't like getting hit) Paintball mask - $15 500 rounds - $5 10,000 round ammo crate - $40 Family discount (3 or more Standard rentals gets 25% off)

Rules of Verdun: no shooting an enemy player within 10 feet within 10 feet either yell "surrender or bang bang" thisindicates that the player is out If both players say "bang bang or surrender" than both players are out Rules of speedball: No surrender rule No "bang bang or surrender" within 10 feet All deadly forces are authorized
